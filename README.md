# A Program Design Homework
A Shawarma Game realized by HUAJI_TYE2007.
Welcome to play and have fun!

## How to Play

1. Compile the program using a C++ compiler that supports C++11 or later.
2. Run the compiled executable.
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to serve customers and manage your shawarma stand.
4. Try to earn as much gold as possible before the day ends!
 - Serve customers by fulfilling their orders for pies, chips, and cola.
 - Manage your resources and stocks wisely.
 - Keep an eye on customer patience levels to avoid losing sales.
 - Be careful with your making process to avoid wasting and annoying customers.
5. Upgrade your stand to improve your service and attract more customers.

Note: This is a simple console-based game. Enjoy the retro gaming experience!
More features and improvements may be added in future versions. Stay tuned!
